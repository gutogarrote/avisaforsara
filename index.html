Sara wants to visit us!

Sara is a front-end Web Developer living in Lebanon. She is also known for her excellent posts and passion about CSS & SVG. She is invited to conferences, so she can spread the word, we can learn and grow, and the whole community of web devs is pushed forwards!

However, sometimes she can't go because... she's denied a visa.

In return, we too are denied the pleasure of receiving her wisdom, the whole community is affected, and a kitten dies. AT LEAST THINK ABOUT THAT KITTEN.

In 2013, Lebanese citizens had visa-free or visa on arrival access to only 38 countries and territories. In the Henley & Partners Visa Restriction Index, a global ranking of countries according to the travel freedom that their citizens enjoy, Lebanon holds the 88th place, with a score of 38. The maximum score is 173.
